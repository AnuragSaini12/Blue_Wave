Get to know us better.

Elite Care Recruitment is one of the leading suppliers of healthcare professionals across the UK. Our dedicated teams work with our qualified candidates to provide the roles they need, where they want to work and when they need them. We are proud of the working relationships that we have built with our locums, allowing them to fit work around their lifestyles.


Elite Care dedicated staffs available 24/7 to our valued Employers, specialising in last minute cover from our extensive database of qualified staff. The team aim to serve the clients with high quality service.


Elite Care Recruitment is run by Two Registered Nurses, who both have many years experience in Nursing as well as an extensive knowledge of the agency business sector. Each one of the managing directors work within the business themselves fulfilling shifts at late notice to ensure that we continually provide the best possible care to all of our clients.

Who we are and what we do

Elite Care Recruitments provides a personally tailored service which strives to meet our clients requirements perfectly. We provide staffing solutions to GP surgeries, private and NHS hospitals and nursing and care homes throughout the UK.
